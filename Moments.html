Every effort has a reward

With God's permission, whatever we strive for, regardless of the reward, we will definitely receive unexpected blessings. This is what happened to me, throughout working for more than 20 years, various experiences, hardships and joys, who knows what we feel.

This moment became history in my life. A sweet memory that will never be forgotten. One day I received a letter from the National Palace, a letter with my name written on it. It was written congratulations for being awarded the 'Darjah Yang Amat Dihormati Setia DiRaja Bentara Setia DiRaja' medal which was written at the end of my name B.S.D.

These beautiful days with my beloved wife who is pregnant. A little worried if she gives birth during the event. Alhamdulillah everything went well. Therefore, don't worry when you are told to work. Every effort will definitely have a reward.

MOHD SASALI BIN ISMAIL B.S.D.
