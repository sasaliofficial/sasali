Skill


VOICE MIXING
Voice premix is focusing on dialogue for initial balancing and panning based on the script or storyboard and director requirements, ability to perform premix so thataudio sound track balanced and ready for final mix process and completed within the date line in accordance with production house requirement









MUSICAL INSTRUMENT PREMIX
Musical instrument premix is focusing on premix for song production, film scoring, movie soundtrack, music for video and jingle production and with good sound judgement skills and ability to evaluate music mix with music producer accordance with recording label requirement.









SOUND EFFECT PREMIX
Sound effect premix is focusing on sound effect for initial balancing and panning based on the script or storyboard and director requirements, ability to perform premix so that audio sound track balanced and ready for final mix process











LIVE SOUND PREMIX
Live sound premix is focusing on live sound for initial balancing and panning based on programme running order requirements, ability to perform premix so that audio sound track balanced and ready for final live sound mix requirement









LOCATION SOUND PREMIX
Location sound premix is focusing on location sound for initial balancing and panning based on the script or storyboard and director requirements, ability to perform premix so that audio sound track balanced and ready for final mix process









RECORDING MEDIA OPERATION
Recording Media Operation involves in identifying, inspecting and packing properly the recording media prior to transporting and preparing on set according to production live or pre recorded shooting requirement in compliance with SOP and Safety Procedure. Repacking of equipment after used is required for safekeeping
